High-Quality Processed Raw Cashew for Industrial and Retail Use

At Yoriyorii Cashew Nuts, we go a step beyond sourcing — we process raw cashew nuts to meet international quality standards. Our processed raw cashews are carefully de-shelled, cleaned, and sun-dried, ready for use in snack production, nut butters, confectionery, and cashew-based beverages.

We work with certified facilities that maintain food safety standards while preserving the natural taste and nutrients of the cashew kernel. Our nuts are available in various grades (WW240, WW320, etc.) and can be packed according to client requirements — vacuum-sealed, bulk bags, or custom packaging.

Whether you're a wholesaler in Europe, a retailer in the U.S., or a processor in Asia, we supply processed raw cashew in any quantity — from a few kilograms to full container loads. Our logistics network ensures safe and prompt delivery to any destination across the globe.
